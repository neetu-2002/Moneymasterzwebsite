Cookie Policy

Version: 1.0
Date: 1 October 2023

‍

1. Introduction

‍
Contiant Ltd. (“we” or “us” or “our”) uses cookies and other related technologies when you visit our website https://www.contiant.com (the “Website”) to help customize it and improve your experience. Cookies are also placed by third parties we have engaged. In the document below we inform you about the use of cookies on our Website. You will also be able to find information on how to control and disable these cookies and withdraw consent to collect them. We reserve the right to make changes to this Policy. You would be notified about any such amendment. Nevertheless, we encourage you to periodically review this Cookie Policy and stay informed of updates.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are those small pieces of data stored in text files saved on your computer or another device when you visit a website. Cookies enable us to record and store your preference, such as login information, user history language settings and other preference for a certain period of time. The information stored therein may be returned to our servers or to the servers of the relevant third parties during a subsequent visit.

3. What types of cookies do we use?

‍
Strictly necessary cookies

‍They are mandatory for the Website to operate correctly. Without them, the Website will either not work at all or will not work properly. These cookies do not track any personal data of visitors, therefore a consent for them is not required.

‍Technical or functional cookies

‍We set these cookies to ensure the proper functioning of certain parts of the Websit and to store your user preferences. This avoids the need to repeatedly enter the same information when visiting our Website. We may place these cookies without your consent.

Statistics or analytical cookies

‍We use statistics cookies to optimize the Website experience for our users. With these statistics cookies we get information about how visitors interact with our Website. We need permission to place statistics cookies.

Marketing/Tracking cookies

Marketing/Tracking cookies are cookies or any other form of local storage, used to create user profiles to display advertising or to track the user on this Website or across several websites for similar marketing purposes.

Session cookies

Session cookies are short- lived cookies that are only stored until the user closes the browser. They are used to identify users while they use the Website.

Persistent cookies

These are cookies with a longer lifespan which remain on the user device until they reach their expiration date or the users clear them from the browser.

Proprietary cookies

They are provided by the website you are visiting. Third- party cookies Third party cookies are cookies that are placed by a website other than the one you are currently using. In cases where we use cookies of this type, we have added the applicable third-party Privacy policy.

4. Consent

‍
Since strictly necessary cookies are required for the proper functioning of the Website, their use does not require the User's consent. Regarding the other types of cookies, you can give your consent to their use in the cookie banner placed on the Website.

You can disable the use of cookies through your browser, but be aware that our Website may not work properly or provide full functionality.

5. How can you manage cookies?

‍
1. Remove cookies from your device
You can delete all cookies on your device by clearing your browsing history in your browser.

2. Manage cookies on a certain website
You can change your internet browser settings so that you receive a message each time a cookie is placed.

3. Blocking cookies
You can block cookies by setting your browser to prevent cookies from being placed on your device, but then you may have to manually adjust some parameters each time you visit a website.
